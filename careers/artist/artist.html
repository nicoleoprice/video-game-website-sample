Types of Artists

    There are many different types of artists. All with different skillsets and use different software. The ones listed below are only a couple of the common ones.

2D/Texture Artists
These artists create textures that can be applied to the things the 3D modellers make. This type of artist requires extensive knowledge in lighting, materials, perspective, and visual effects.
3D Modellers
This type of artist will build characters, objects, and the environment with 3D modelling software. This includes life forms, scenery, furniture, plants, and more.
Concept Artist
This type of artist sketches the ideas for the world of the game. They do not typically use software for their art, so it is usually a pen and paper. They sketch out ideas of everything ranging from characters to plants to clothing. Although their art is not displayed in the final product, they help create the foundations of the game.
Effects Artist
This artist will work with both 2D and 3D software, particle systems and lights, to create lively effects. Some of the effects this artist will make are weather effects, water leaks, smoke, blowing dust, and more.
Environmental Artists
This artist only works on the environment itself. They may do a variety of tasks, such as 3D modeling, work with complex layered shaders, simple animations, and texturing.
Lighting Artists
This type of artist will place all of the lights in the game, adjust the color, adjust the intensity, and how the light falls off. This is an important job because it helps create the mood of the scenery and helps the world look more realistic.
