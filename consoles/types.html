Types of Gaming

PC Gaming

    Personal Computer Gaming, or PC Gaming, is the process of playing a video game on a computer, which can be a desktop or a laptop.

Console Gaming

    Console Gaming is the process of playing a video game on a system that displays a visual on screen and allows the player to move around with a controller or joy-stick.

VR/AR Gaming

    Virtual Reality Gaming, or VR Gaming, is first-person gaming where the player is immersed into the game and typically requires physical movement to move the character. For example, a player might have to move their leg to kick a ball in the game. On the other hand, Augmented Reality Gaming, or AR Gaming, is gaming where digital influences are added to real life.
