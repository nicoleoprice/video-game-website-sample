Video Games

Welcome!

    If you have found this website, you must be interested in video games! If you are unfamiliar with video games, this site can still be useful since technical terms are not discussed and the information is generalized. So, what is a video game? A video game is an electronic game played by manipulating images produced by a computer program. You also may be wondering, is videogames one or two words? Both are perfectly acceptable.
